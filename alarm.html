Application developed as a personal project.

Do you like TV Show themes? Do you use alarm apps? So how about wake up the your favorite TV Shows theme song as alarm!

- Choose from a variaty of TV Shows or leave them all selected and enjoy unknown TV Shows that you might like it.
- Didn't like some theme that played? You can "dislike/block" it and it will never play as your alarm again.
- Use "Ok Google" voice command to create alarms without going to the app.
- Didn't find some TV Show that you like? Suggest new TV Show themes to be added.
- After some minutes playing the TV Show theme song, the app will start playing the default alarm. This ensures that you don't miss the alarm.
- Feel free to give us feedback, so we can improve the app quality =D




Privacy Policy

This application does not collect any personal or sensitive information from the user. Bellow you have the explanation for all permissions needed in our applications:

Read phone status and identity: Permission used to check if you are receiving a call or currently on a call. So that our app does not play any sound in this situation.
Receive data from Internet/View network connections/Full network access: Permission used to download TV Show themes and images so that your alarm rings even if your connection is off.
Run at startup: Permission used so your alarms are scheduled after a restart is completed.
